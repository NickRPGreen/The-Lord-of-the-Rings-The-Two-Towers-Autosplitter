# The-Lord-of-the-Rings-The-Two-Towers-Autosplitter
ASL and Blank Splits for autosplitting the GameCube version of The Lord of the Rings: The Two Towers on Dolphin emulators.

ASL Includes:
* Starts on skipping the Prologue's opening cutscene for Aragorn, or selecting a character and hitting continue for Legolas and Gimli
* Splits on selecting your character and hitting continue for the next level
* Splits on killing the final troll and the final custscene starting in Honburg Courtyard
* Mid-level splits are currently not supported

Requires emu-help-v3 to be added to your LiveSplit\Components folder: https://github.com/Jujstme/emu-help-v3

Also available are blank split files for Aragon and Legolas/Gimli/Isildur.
